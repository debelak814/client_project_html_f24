Blue & Maize Cupboard

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering a wide array of resources and services, including making sure that students have access to food. The Blue & Maize Cupboard is a student-run food pantry that provides free food and other essentials to students in need. The pantry is located in the School of Social Work Building and is open to all UMSI students. The Blue & Maize Cupboard is a great resource for students who are experiencing food insecurity or who are struggling to make ends meet. If you are in need of food assistance, please visit the Blue & Maize Cupboard during their regular hours of operation. You can view more information about their services at the Blue & Maize Cupboard website.

Common FAQ's per the Blue & Maize Cupboards website

  • Who do they help?
    Anyone with a valid University of Michigan ID.
  • What if I'm just a little short on food?
    Go anyway! You can even just look around to figure out if you need anything.
  • Does going impact financial aid?
    No!
